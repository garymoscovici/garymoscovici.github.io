About Me

Hello, I am Saul, an animation artist who loves the honesty of pencil lines and the rhythm of hand-drawn motion. My work focuses on character-driven stories, quiet observational drawing, and the textures of everyday life.

When I am not animating, you will find me filling sketchbooks on park benches or studying light across rooftops.
